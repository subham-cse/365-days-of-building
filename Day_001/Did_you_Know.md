## Did You Know? – Day 001

- The New Year begins on January 1 because of the Roman calendar reform by Julius Caesar in 45 BCE.

- Earth takes about 365.2422 days to orbit the Sun, which is why we need a leap year every 4 years.

- Your brain forms new habits more easily at the beginning of a year due to a psychological effect called the “fresh start effect”.

- GitHub tracks contribution streaks daily, which motivates consistency through visual feedback.

- Writing down goals increases the chance of achieving them by more than 40%.
